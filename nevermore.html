After spending the entire 2018 year looking for a job, and to no avail, I was about to leave it aside for a while, when suddenly one of my interviews went well, and I was called to work at Nova Software. For a large part of the clients, being computer lay people, many have doubts and difficulties to use the system, so Nova Software hired a group of people to provide support for these clients, and that's where I came in.

Sistema Nova

At first we discussed whether this was a viable thing, considering that our knowledge was not great in this area. Douglas and Lucas, who eventually joined the project later, kind of abandoned it, but I did, and I created the whole front end, after that, I learned and applied the database and PHP.
